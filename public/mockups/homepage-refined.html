CommBank Bids to Ease Customer Fears Over AI Adoption

Commonwealth Bank of Australia has partnered with Melbourne Business School to launch the AI Attitudes Barometer, a comprehensive study measuring public sentiment toward AI adoption in banking and financial services.

The initiative aims to address growing concerns among consumers about how artificial intelligence is being used to make decisions that affect their financial lives, from credit approvals to fraud detection.
